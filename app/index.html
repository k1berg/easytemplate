Our story

It all started in a London hair salon in 2004...

Back in 2004 a small hair salon in east London needed something to help manage its daily business with the owner too busy cutting hair, managing the hair salon and ensuring clients where satisfied.

Then partners, Paul (who looked after marketing for the salon and our founder) & Bradley (the hair stylist) decided to make the leap to start using salon software, along with 24/7 online appointment booking to manage their scheduling, inventory management and staff commissions.

Now 8 years on, that salon is the largest independent luxury salon brand in London operating from four prestige locations around the city. Part of this success is partly due to having the insight which allowed its owners to make the right decisions.

From our usage of other salon systems we have put this knowledge together with some fantastic software developers into building an easy to use and making salon software free for any beauty & wellness business – whatever their size and wherever they are in the world.

Got more questions about our salon booking software?

Here are some answers to your most common questions.

Is there anything to install?

Nothing! Yes, you read correctly. We like to keep things simple at Belliata and that means you can stop looking for hair salon software free downloads. Our cloud based solution means you don’t have to worry about anything, as we look after all of the upgrades for you.

Can I use my existing computer or tablet?

Yes. Our solution can be used on Windows, Apple and Android devices without any issues. All you need is an Internet connection and a web browser, such as Chrome or Safari. What can be more simple?

Who owns my data?

You own your customer data and we are working on allowing you to back up your data automatically with Dropbox or Google Drive, so you have extra reassurance. We realize that data is now an essential element to any business.

How can I get support?

We aim to provide you with the best free support to ensure you get the maximum benefit from Belliata Salon Software. We are available 7 days a week and aim to respond to you within 4 hours. If onboarding, live chat, email and YouTube videos to guide you through.

Is my data safe?

We use SSL (look for the padlock on the address bar of your browser) for our website, which is hosted by Amazon (yes the huge online store) Web Services. . As we are based in Europe, we have the strongest data protection rules in the world and have to follow tight guidelines about customer data.

Can I cancel at anytime?

Yes you can downgrade and cancel our premium salon & spa software plans at any time. But don’t forget you can always have access to our free salon software if your salon needs to change.
